Everything Verby can do

From AI-powered dictation to email generation and smart prompt crafting, Verby turns your voice into polished text across every app on your desktop.

Core Feature

AI-Enhanced Dictation

Hold a key and speak naturally. Verby transcribes your voice using OpenAI Whisper, then enhances the output with AI to produce clear, well-structured text. No manual editing required — just talk, and Verby delivers polished text in seconds.

On Mac, hold the Fn key to activate AI-enhanced dictation. On Windows, hold CapsLock. Verby listens while you hold the key and processes your speech the moment you release it. The result is not a raw transcript — it is an AI-refined version of what you said, restructured for clarity and readability.

Mac Hotkey

Hold Fn to speak

Windows Hotkey

Hold CapsLock to speak

Powered By

OpenAI Whisper transcription

Email Generation

Say "email John about the project deadline" and Verby generates a complete, ready-to-send email. It detects email intent automatically from your speech — no need to switch modes or select a template. Just speak naturally and Verby handles the formatting, tone, greeting, and sign-off.

The generated email matches a professional tone while preserving your intent. Verby works seamlessly with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, and any other email client. The email is injected directly at your cursor, so you can review it and hit send immediately.

Smart Prompt Crafting

Verby converts your casual, spoken instructions into structured AI prompts. Say something like "build me a habit tracker app" and Verby transforms it into a detailed prompt with context, requirements, and constraints — ready to paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or VS Code Copilot.

Verby detects the task type automatically. Whether you are asking for code, a piece of writing, data analysis, or creative work, the prompt is structured to match. This means you get better results from AI tools without spending time manually crafting prompts.

Speech Cleanup

Sometimes you just need a clean transcript of what you said, not an AI-rewritten version. Speech cleanup mode transcribes your voice and removes filler words, false starts, and grammatical errors while keeping your natural voice and original meaning intact.

On Mac, hold Ctrl to activate speech cleanup. On Windows, hold Right Ctrl. This is ideal for drafting messages, taking notes, or writing first drafts where you want the text to sound like you — just cleaner.

Hold Ctrl to speak

Windows Hotkey

Hold Right Ctrl to speak

System-Wide Text Injection

Verby injects the generated text directly at your cursor position in any application. There is no copy-paste step, no intermediate window, and no clipboard involved. The text simply appears where you are typing, as if you had written it by hand.

This works across browsers, code editors, email clients, chat applications, document editors, and terminals. Whether you are composing an email in Gmail, writing code in VS Code, or chatting in Slack, Verby places the text exactly where you need it.

If you are in VS Code working on a Python project, Verby generates code-oriented prompts with the right language context. If you are in Gmail, it defaults to email formatting. The output adapts to your environment so you can stay focused on your work.

Pattern Learning

Verby learns your prompt style, vocabulary, and preferences over time. The more you use it, the better it becomes at generating text that matches the way you communicate. This means fewer edits and more accurate output with every use.

Pattern learning applies across all modes — dictation, email generation, and prompt crafting. Verby adapts to your writing style so the output feels natural and personal rather than generic.

Verby is a system-wide desktop application, not a browser extension or a plugin. It works in every application where you can type text. Gmail, Slack, VS Code, ChatGPT, Notion, Microsoft Word, Terminal — if your cursor is there, Verby can inject text into it.
