## 4. Page-by-Page Recommendations

### Homepage (/) -- PRIORITY: HIGH

**Title Tag:**
- BEFORE: `Verby -- Free AI Voice-to-Text App for Mac & Windows`
- AFTER: `Verby -- Free AI Voice Dictation & Speech-to-Text App for Mac & Windows`
- WHY: Adds "voice dictation" and "speech-to-text" -- two of the highest-volume search terms competitors rank for. Slightly longer but within 60-char sweet spot when Google truncates.

**Meta Description:**
- BEFORE: `Talk to any app. Verby turns your voice into emails, AI prompts, Reddit comments, social replies, and clean text -- free for Mac. Try 20 free dictations today. 60-second setup.`
- AFTER: `Free AI voice dictation for Mac & Windows. Verby turns speech into emails, AI prompts, and clean text -- injected at your cursor in any app. Try 20 free dictations today.`
- WHY: Front-loads "Free AI voice dictation" for snippet visibility. Adds "speech" as a natural variation. Keeps under 160 chars (158). Removes Reddit/social mentions (niche) in favor of broader appeal.

**Meta Keywords:**
- BEFORE: `voice to text app, AI dictation software, speech to text desktop, voice typing Mac, voice typing Windows, AI email generator, voice prompt tool, hands free typing`
- AFTER: `voice to text app, AI dictation software, speech to text desktop app, voice typing Mac, voice typing Windows, AI email generator, voice dictation software, free dictation app, speech recognition software, best dictation app Mac, talk to type app, Whisper dictation`
- WHY: Adds "voice dictation software", "free dictation app", "speech recognition software", "best dictation app Mac", "talk to type app", and "Whisper dictation" -- all high-volume terms competitors rank for.

**H1:**
- BEFORE: `AI Voice-to-Text App for Mac & Windows`
- AFTER: `AI Voice Dictation & Speech-to-Text for Mac & Windows` (or keep current -- it's already strong)
- WHY: Optional change. Adding "dictation" and "speech-to-text" captures both keyword variants.

## 6. Priority Actions

### Immediate (Do This Week)

1. **Fix Help page title, description, H1, and keywords** -- This is the single biggest SEO gap on the entire site. The help page is invisible to search engines for any informational query.

2. **Fix Features page H1** -- Change "Everything Verby can do" to "AI Voice Dictation Features & Capabilities". This is a zero-effort, high-impact change.

3. **Fix Pricing page H1** -- Change "Simple, transparent pricing" to "Voice Dictation Pricing -- Free & Pro Plans".

4. **Fix Download page meta description** -- Currently 169 chars, gets truncated. Shorten to recommended version.

5. **Fix About page meta description** -- Currently 184 chars, gets truncated. Shorten to recommended version.

### Short-Term (This Month)

6. **Add "speech-to-text" and "voice dictation" to Homepage title** -- Currently only has "voice-to-text". Adding both variants captures a much wider search net.

7. **Expand meta keywords on all pages** -- Most pages have 4-6 keywords. Competitors use 8-12. Add the specific terms listed above.

8. **Update help-content.json category headings** -- Ensure the FAQ/help article category names include keywords (see Help page recommendations above).

### Medium-Term (Next 30 Days)

9. **Write comparison blog posts** -- "Verby vs Wispr Flow" and "Verby vs Voicy" are missing. Competitors write comparison pages against each other (Wispr has a "superwhisper-alternative" page). Verby should have comparison pages for all major competitors.

10. **Create a dedicated "Speech to Text" landing page** -- Competitors like Voicy have `/windows-speech-to-text` as a standalone landing page. Verby should create `/speech-to-text` targeting that high-volume keyword.

11. **Add "offline" and "privacy" messaging where applicable** -- Multiple competitors (Superwhisper, Handy, VoiceInk, SpeakMac) heavily emphasize offline/privacy. If Verby has any local processing, highlight it.
