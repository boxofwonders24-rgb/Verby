How it works

🎙

Three Input Modes

Hold a key for AI-enhanced prompts & emails, or speech cleanup. System-wide hotkeys on Mac (Fn, Ctrl) and Windows (CapsLock, Right Ctrl). Works in any app.

✉️

Email Generation

Say "email John about..." and get a ready-to-send email that matches your natural tone. No templates, no forms.

📍

Context Aware

Auto-detects your current app and project. Prompts are tailored to what you're working on right now.

⚡

Instant Injection

Text appears at your cursor — ChatGPT, Slack, VS Code, email. No copy-paste.

🧹

Speech Cleanup

Hold Ctrl to dictate. Verby cleans up grammar and filler words while keeping your natural voice.

🔄

Auto-Updates

New features and improvements delivered automatically. No manual downloads needed.
