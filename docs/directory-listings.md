**First Comment** (maker's comment):
```
Hey everyone — I'm Stephen, the solo dev behind Verby.

I built this because I was frustrated with how slow typing felt compared to just saying what I meant. I use AI tools constantly — ChatGPT, Claude, various coding assistants — and I kept catching myself spending more time crafting the prompt than the actual task would take. I also wrote dozens of emails a day and they all started the same way: stare at the blank compose window, type a few words, delete them, try again. So I built something where I could just hold a key and talk. Verby takes my messy spoken thoughts and turns them into clean emails, structured prompts, or just tidied-up text, then drops it wherever my cursor is.

The thing that surprised me most was how many people started using it for Reddit and social media replies. Turns out a lot of people want to respond to comments but don't feel like typing out a paragraph on their phone or getting into a back-and-forth. With Verby you just say "reply to this, I tried that and it worked great, here's what I did differently" and it writes something that sounds like a real person on that platform. That wasn't the original plan but it became one of the most-used features.

It's free to try — 20 dictations a day, no account needed. Would love to hear what you think.
```
